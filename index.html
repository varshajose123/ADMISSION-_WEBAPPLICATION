PhD in MUJ

OVERVIEW

The MUJ PhD is modelled on the new UGC guidelines of PhD Programs. The University envisages high standard of research so that the PhD degree is also of high standard. The objectives of this are to impart research training to the scholar and prepare him/her to carry on independent investigation and original research and to enable them to make a significant contribution to knowledge in their respective field and profession. The training shall ensure that it is not an end in itself, but a means for a fruitful research career. Presently, MUJ is providing admission to the PhD for full-time& part-time research in almost all subjects in Engineering, Sciences, Management, Commerce, Humanities and Social Sciences.
